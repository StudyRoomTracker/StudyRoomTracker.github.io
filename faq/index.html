    Frequently Asked Questions

    How do I occupy a room?

     You have to log in before being able to occupy a room. When logged in, navigate to a floor and click on a room with a green outline. A button should appear, and click on that. One user can only occupy one room at a single time.


    How do I unoccupy a room?

     You can only unoccupy a room you occupied previously (with that account). If you forget to unoccupy, the room you occupied will be automatically unoccupied after 2 hours of inactivity.


    How do I log in?

     Click the green "Login" button at the top right of the screen. On the popup, type in a valid username (x@y.z) and a password and click the login button. To switch accounts, click the green button again and do the process again.


    What am I looking at on a floor page?

     On any of the floor pages there is a floor plan of the respective floor. Study rooms are outlined with red or green to indicate their availability. When a room is occupied (or unavailable) it will be red; when it is available it will be green.
